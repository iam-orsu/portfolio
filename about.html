Stop Being Stupid About Your Security

If you don’t care about your client's data, If you’re too lazy to pay attention, We’re on your ass 24/7, fixing your pathetic security. Here are some case studies of businesses that didn’t take security seriously and got burned..

  • Twilio (August 2022)

    Twilio experienced a breach after employees fell victim to a sophisticated social engineering attack. Hackers impersonated Twilio's IT department, prompting employees to enter credentials on a fake sign-in page, leading to unauthorized access to internal systems and data of 125 customers. (Reference)

  • Caesars Entertainment (2023)

    Caesars Entertainment experienced a cyberattack where hackers used social engineering tactics to infiltrate their network. The breach resulted in unauthorized access to customer data, highlighting the critical need for robust security measures. (Reference)

  • Australian Banks (December 2024)

    Australian banking apps, including those from the top four banks, were targeted in a sophisticated global malware attack. Criminals impersonated job recruiters, prompting victims to download a fake CRM app that installed malware designed to steal credentials from banking and other critical applications. (Reference)
